THE SCIENCE

Why These Pairings Work

Great flavor combinations share specific molecular properties. FlavorLab identifies them instantly.

🔬

Shared Aroma Compounds

Ingredients sharing volatile aromatic molecules create natural harmony. When your brain detects familiar compounds from two sources, it registers them as belonging together.

⚖️

Complementary Taste Profiles

Sweet, acid, fat, salt, and bitter notes each serve a role. The best pairings balance these profiles so no single taste dominates — every sip or bite is in harmony.

✨

Balanced Sensory Contrast

Opposing flavor profiles don't clash — they amplify. A touch of bitter enhances sweetness. A hint of heat deepens richness. Contrast, done right, makes both ingredients shine.
